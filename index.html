About Me

Personal Statement

I graduated from medical faculty with the deep sense of focus in treating patients from rural and penurious areas in Sri Lanka. Currently I am working as a Senior Registrar in Paediatrics while improving my skills and knowledge. I have been treating children for the past 9 years of my medical carrier.

Most of the ailing illnesses I have seen were preventable or could have presented before if better health awareness and resources were available. These experiences have strengthened my belief in importance of enhancing my expertise in Paediatrics care to ensure delivery of high-quality compassionate health care for every child in need.

Career Objectives

  • Completion of Stage 5, Overseas training of MD Paediatrics program, University of Colombo, Sri Lanka to become a board-certified Paediatrician
  • Contribution to research activities and audits to improve health system of Sri Lanka and Globally
  • Providing medical education to both health staff and community to enhance quality of paediatric health
